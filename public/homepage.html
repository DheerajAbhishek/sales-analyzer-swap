How We Use Your Data

Restalytics is designed with transparency and data security at its core. We only access and process the data necessary to provide you with valuable business insights.

  • Process sales reports and transaction data for analytics
  • Extract business-relevant emails from your Gmail (with explicit permission)
  • Generate automated reports and insights for your restaurant
  • Provide real-time dashboard analytics and visualizations
  • Send you weekly business insights and performance summaries

We never share, sell, or misuse your business data. All data processing is performed solely to provide our analytics services to help you make better business decisions.
